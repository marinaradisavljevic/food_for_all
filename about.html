About

The Food for all project was born out of the fact that many people decide to leave their pets on the street because they are financially unable to take care of them. Food for all uses the good will of the pet owners to help each other by connecting the owners willing to pay for more pet food than they need and the pet owners in need of help to feed their pets. The project has two types of users: the pet owners who are given an ID number and a list of pet shops with food stocks available, and the pet shops willing to be a part of the project who enable the purchase of surplus food and distribute it to the registered pet owners. We hope that the food distribution system will only be the first step in helping to keep the pets off the streets and in solving the problem of stray animals.
